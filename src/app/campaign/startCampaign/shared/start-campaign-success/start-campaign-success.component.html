Todah Success Tool Questions:


1. Do you have active followers on your Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn?

2. Do you have at least 20% of your goal pre-committed?

3. Will you target 90% more people than you need to reach your goal?

4. Do you have a communication/marketing plan for your campaign?

5. Do you have a video to promote your campaign?

6. Do you have pictures for your campaign?

7. Do you have a thank you plan for your donors?

8. Will you be posting 3-5 times a day on your social media to promote your campaign, updates, and general info about your work with the link to the campaign?

9. Do 75% or more of your donors give online?

10. Will you spend at least a month preparing for the public launch of your campaign?

11. Have you built a team to help promote the campaign through various channels?
